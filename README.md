# 🪳 Cockroach Killer Game

A fun, endless browser game where you hunt down cockroaches with your trusty swatter!

## Features

- 🎯 Click to kill moving cockroaches
- 📊 Score tracking with high score persistence
- 🔥 Combo system for consecutive kills
- 🎵 Sound effects (can be muted)
- 📈 Increasing difficulty as you progress
- 💯 Fully client-side - no server needed!

## How to Play

1. Open the game in your browser
2. Click on the cockroaches as they scurry around
3. Build combos by killing multiple cockroaches quickly
4. Try to beat your high score!

The game gets progressively harder as your score increases - cockroaches spawn faster and move more frequently!
